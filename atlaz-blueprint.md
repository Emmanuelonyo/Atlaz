# **1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

## **Company Overview**

**Atlaz** is a business-setup and operations-enablement company offering **end-to-end incorporation, compliance, and virtual-office solutions** for entrepreneurs and startups in **Nigeria and globally**, including **US company formation for non-US residents**.

## **Mission**

To empower entrepreneurs anywhere in the world—especially within emerging markets—to launch, formalize, and scale credible businesses through seamless incorporation, compliance, and operational support.

## **Vision**

To become Africa’s most trusted global business-formation platform, enabling borderless entrepreneurship and powering the next generation of global-first African startups.

## **Problem Atlaz Solves**

Entrepreneurs in Nigeria and abroad face significant hurdles:

- Complex **CAC registration** and documentation requirements.
- Mandatory **registered office address** requirements even for small businesses.
- Difficulty obtaining **TIN**, opening corporate bank accounts, and maintaining compliance.
- Lack of access to professional legal/administrative support and startup resources.
- For global founders: challenges forming **US LLCs**, obtaining **EIN**, registered-agent services, compliance filings, and accessing US online payment tools.

## **Core Services Overview**

- **Nigeria Entity Formation** (Business Name, Limited Company)
- **Nigeria Virtual Office / Business Address**
- **Post-Incorporation Support** (TIN, bank account guidance, compliance alerts)
- **US LLC / C-Corp Formation for Non-Residents**
- **US Registered-Agent + Virtual Address**
- **Mail handling & forwarding** (Nigeria + US)
- **Startup Kit** (domain, email, cloud credits, compliance onboarding)

## **Target Market**

- Nigerian entrepreneurs & SMEs
- Diaspora Nigerians launching businesses remotely
- Foreigners seeking Nigerian market entry
- Tech startups seeking US investor compatibility
- Freelancers needing professional addresses for invoicing
- Global entrepreneurs seeking US presence

## **Value Proposition**

Atlaz provides a **one-stop, fully managed, cross-border business-setup and compliance platform**, eliminating complexity and delivering credibility, speed, virtual-office infrastructure, and expert support.

# **2. MARKET & NEEDS ANALYSIS**

## **Pain Points for Nigerian Entrepreneurs**

According to Nigerian legal guidance on business registration:

- CAC registration requires **name search/reservation, documentation, valid address, director KYC, and filings**, which overwhelm many founders.
- Registered office address **must be valid and usable for correspondence**, making virtual addresses essential.
- After incorporation, founders struggle to obtain **TIN**, bank accounts, and navigate compliance.
- Lack of resources for **business email, domain, brand identity**, and corporate professionalism.

## **Pain Points for Diaspora / Foreigners**

- Need trusted agents to establish and maintain businesses in Nigeria.
- Difficulty navigating Nigerian compliance remotely.
- For US incorporation: non-residents struggle with:

  - Getting EIN without SSN.
  - Finding a US registered agent.
  - Understanding tax/reporting obligations.

# **4. LEGAL, OPERATIONAL & COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK**

# **4.1 Nigeria Compliance Rules**

- CAC registration requires name reservation, forms, identity documents, and valid local address
- Registered address can be virtual.
- Post-incorporation: TIN issuance and annual returns mandatory

---

# **4.2 US Compliance Requirements**

- Non-US residents can own US LLCs without SSN.
- Registered agent with physical address required.
- Articles of Organization + Operating Agreement required.
- Foreign-owned LLCs must file IRS forms (incl. Form 5472). Penalties apply for non-compliance.

# **A. LLC vs C-Corporation Comparison**

| Feature                 | LLC                                             | C-Corporation                     |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **Best For**            | Freelancers, small businesses, foreign founders | Tech startups planning VC funding |
| **Taxation**            | Pass-through (no corporate tax unless elected)  | Corporate tax + shareholder tax   |
| **Complexity**          | Low                                             | High                              |
| **Investor Preference** | Low                                             | Very high (VC standard)           |
| **Compliance**          | Simple                                          | Strict (shares, board, minutes)   |

**Recommendation:**

- Use **LLC** unless planning to raise VC or issue stock options.
- Most foreign founders start with an LLC for simplicity, then convert.
